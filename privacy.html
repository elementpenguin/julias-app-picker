Privacy Policy — J.U.L.I.A.S.

Last updated: [July 4, 2026]

J.U.L.I.A.S. ("the app") is a personal directory app that lets you view and manage location, contact, and system information stored in a Google Sheet that you own.

What data the app accesses

When you sign in with Google, the app requests access to:

  • Files you create or select (Google Drive "drive.file" scope): the app can only see and edit a Google Sheet that either (a) it creates for you directly, or (b) you explicitly choose using Google's own file picker. The app cannot see, list, or access any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Google Sheets ("spreadsheets" scope): used to read and write the rows of location/contact data you enter into that one sheet.

Where your data is stored

All data you enter into the app — addresses, contact names, phone numbers, emails, notes, and any other fields — is stored directly in the Google Sheet in your own Google Drive account. The app does not operate a separate server or database, and does not copy, store, or retain your data anywhere outside of that spreadsheet.

What the developer can and cannot see

The developer of this app has no access to your Google account, your Google Sheet, or any data stored in it. Because there is no backend server, the developer never receives, stores, or processes your data at any point.

Data sharing

The app does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party. The only parties involved in handling your data are you (via your own Google account and Google Sheet) and Google's own APIs, which the app communicates with directly using your authorization.

Advertising

The app contains no advertising and does not use your data for advertising purposes.

Revoking access

You can revoke the app's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access. Revoking access does not delete your existing Google Sheet or its data — it only stops the app from being able to read or edit it going forward.
